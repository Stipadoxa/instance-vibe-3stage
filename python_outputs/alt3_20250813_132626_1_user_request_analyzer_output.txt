## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain**: E-commerce
2. **Platform**: Mobile
3. **User Goal**: The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose**: To display comprehensive information about a specific used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed decision about purchasing it.

**Required Information & Actions**:

* **Heading**: 2018 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks**:
    * **Images**: Carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showcasing the laptop from different angles (including any visible wear and tear).
    * **Price**: $800
    * **Condition**: Excellent - Minor scratches on the bottom case. Fully functional.
    * **Seller Profile:**  Avatar of seller (John Doe), 4.8 star rating (123 reviews), Seller Name: John Doe.
    * **Description**: "Selling my well-maintained 2018 MacBook Pro 13-inch.  It's in excellent condition with only minor scratches on the bottom.  Comes with original charger.  Battery health is 85%."
    * **Specifications**:
        * Processor: 2.3 GHz Intel Core i5
        * Memory: 8GB RAM
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey (upgradable)
    * **Shipping Information**: Free shipping within the US.


* **Primary & Secondary Actions**:
    * **Primary Action**:  Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary Action**:  Add to Favorites (Button/Icon)


* **Required Status Indicators**:
    * Last Updated: October 26, 2023, 10:30 AM


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Top Navigation**:
* Back (Icon: Left-pointing arrow)

**Bottom Navigation**:
* Home (Icon: House)
* Search (Icon: Magnifying glass)
* Messages (Icon: Speech bubble)
