## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive details about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Image Carousel:**  (Minimum 4 high-quality images showing different angles of the laptop, including screen, keyboard, and ports.  Images should be zoomable.)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent - Minor scratches on the bottom casing. Fully functional.
    * **Seller Profile:**  
        * Profile Picture: [Image of Seller Profile Picture]
        * Seller Name: John Smith
        * Seller Rating: 4.8 stars (123 reviews)
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey
    * **Description:**  "Used MacBook Pro in excellent condition.  Minor scratches on the bottom casing, barely noticeable. Battery health is at 85%. Comes with original charger.  Selling because I upgraded to a newer model."
    * **Shipping Information:**  Shipping calculated at checkout. Seller offers free shipping within the US.


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary Action:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary Action:**  Add to Wishlist (Button)
    * **Secondary Action:** Report Listing (Button - only visible if user taps the three dots icon)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Listing Updated:** Last updated: 2 hours ago
