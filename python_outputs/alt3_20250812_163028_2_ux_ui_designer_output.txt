# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Component Intelligence Applied

- **Typography Hierarchy**:  The `Headline/Large` style is used for the product title to emphasize its importance.  `Body/Medium` is used for the description and specifications to ensure readability. `Title/Medium` style is for section headers (Specifications, Seller), while `Caption/Small` is reserved for secondary information like Listing ID and date posted.

- **Spacing System**:  `itemSpacing` of 16 is used between major sections to create visual breathing room.  `itemSpacing` of 8 is used within sections for closely related items.  Padding is used consistently according to the spacing patterns.

- **Component Behavior**: The `appbar` component displays the title and price; the `avatar` component showcases the seller's profile picture; image galleries are used to present product images; buttons are used consistently for primary actions.

- **Color Application**:  Primary green (`Primary/primary50: #00b53f`) is used for CTAs to highlight important actions. Neutral colors are used consistently for text and background, to ensure visual clarity and hierarchy.  Error state colors would be used if needed according to the design system.

- **Layout Strategy**: A vertical layout is used for the overall page structure, guided by mobile-first design, moving from general information to specific details, primary actions at the end.

## Domain Context Integration

- **Industry Patterns**: The design follows e-commerce conventions by clearly displaying the product title, price, images, description, specifications, and seller information, with clear CTAs.

- **User Mental Models**: The top-down arrangement of information aligns with how users expect to see product details. Key information is given at the top and details are progressively revealed below.

- **Contextual Adaptations**: The design adapts to the context of used laptop sales by including a condition description and images of the laptop.
