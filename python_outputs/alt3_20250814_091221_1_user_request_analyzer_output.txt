## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before deciding whether to contact the seller.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display all relevant information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed decision about contacting the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Product Photos:**  A carousel of at least 4 high-quality photos showing different angles of the laptop (e.g., front, back, keyboard, ports).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition Details:** Excellent - Minor scratches on the bottom case. Fully functional.  Battery health: 85%.
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Username:**  @TechSavvySeller
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5) - 123 reviews
    * **Description:**  Selling my well-maintained 2015 MacBook Pro 13".  It's in excellent condition with minor cosmetic wear (scratches on the bottom).  Battery health is at 85%.  Comes with original charger. Runs smoothly and has been meticulously cared for.  Perfect for students or professionals.
    * **Specifications:** (This section can be collapsible for space saving)
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey


* **Primary Actions:**
    * Contact Seller (Button)

* **Secondary Actions:**
    * Add to Favorites (Heart icon)
    * Report Listing (Flag icon)


* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * "Last updated: 2 hours ago" (if applicable)
    * "Active Listing" (if applicable)


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Top Navigation:**

* Back (Left-aligned, arrow icon)


**Bottom Navigation:**

* Home (House icon)
* Search (Magnifying glass icon)
* Favorites (Heart icon)
