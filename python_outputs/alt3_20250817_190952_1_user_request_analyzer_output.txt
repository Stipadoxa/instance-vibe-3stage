## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** To view the details of a used laptop listing and potentially contact the seller to purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive details about a used laptop listing, enabling users to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro - 13-inch - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Images:** Carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showing different angles of the laptop (e.g., front, back, keyboard, ports).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent - Minor scratches on the bottom casing.  Fully functional.
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Smith
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5.0)  (12 Reviews)
    * **Description:** "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor cosmetic wear.  Includes original charger.  Runs smoothly and has plenty of storage.  Battery health at 85%."
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:** Add to Watchlist (Button)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Listing Posted:** Listed 3 days ago


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Mobile App Navigation:**

* **Top Navigation:**
    * Back (Icon: Left-pointing arrow)
    * Share (Icon: Share icon)

* **Bottom Navigation:**
    * Home (Icon: House icon)
    * Search (Icon: Magnifying glass icon)
    * Watchlist (Icon: Star icon)
    * Profile (Icon: Person icon)

**Note:**  If no data is available for certain fields (e.g., Seller Rating, Specifications),  a clear and concise message should be displayed instead. For example: "Seller has no ratings yet" or "Specifications not provided by seller".  The image carousel should show a placeholder image if no images are uploaded by the seller.
