- **Typography Hierarchy**: Headline/Large is used for the main product title to ensure prominence. Headline/Medium is used for section headers (like "Specifications"), and Body/Medium for descriptions and specifications details.  Caption/Small is used for the "Updated" timestamp.
- **Spacing System**:  The three-container architecture ensures top and bottom navigation bars touch the screen edges.  `itemSpacing` of 16 is used for major section divisions within the content area, and 8 for closer related items. Padding within the main content container is set to 16 for standard spacing.
- **Component Behavior**: The `image` component is used to display the image carousel. The Button component displays the "Contact Seller" button.  The list-item component is used for the specifications.
- **Color Application**: Primary/primary50 is used for the price to highlight its importance.  Neutral/neutral90 is used for primary text, and Neutral/neutral50 for secondary text.
- **Layout Strategy**:  A three-container vertical layout (app bar, content, bottom navigation) is used for optimal mobile UX.  Horizontal layouts are used to arrange related elements (price and update timestamp, seller avatar and rating).

## Domain Context Integration
- **Industry Patterns**: The design follows e-commerce conventions by prominently displaying product images, price, condition, seller information, and a clear call to action ("Contact Seller").
- **User Mental Models**: The layout is organized to support users’ expectations for product detail pages, presenting information in a logical and easily scannable manner.
- **Contextual Adaptations**: The app bar is customized to only include a back button and title for this specific screen.
