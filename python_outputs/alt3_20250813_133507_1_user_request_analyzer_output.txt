## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. This belongs to the **E-commerce** domain.
2. The platform context is **mobile**.
3. The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app to potentially purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose**: To display detailed information about a used laptop for sale, allowing the user to assess its condition, seller reliability, and decide whether to contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions**:

* **Heading**:  2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks**:

    * **Gallery:**  (Carousel of at least 3 high-quality images showing the laptop from different angles, including condition details.  Images should be zoomable.)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent. Minor scuffs on the bottom case, otherwise in perfect working order.
    * **Seller Profile:**  (Displays a small circular profile picture and seller's username:  "TechSavvyTom")  Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 stars)  (123 reviews)
    * **Description:** "Selling my well-maintained 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch.  It's been used for light tasks only and has always been kept in a protective case.  Includes original charger.  Battery health is at 85%."
    * **Specifications:** (collapsible section)
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey (upgradable)


* **Primary & Secondary Actions**:

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  Report Listing (Button, small, at the bottom of the page)

* **Required Status Indicators**:

    * "Last updated: 1 hour ago" (Located below the price)
