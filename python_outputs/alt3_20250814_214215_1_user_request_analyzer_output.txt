## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive information about a used laptop listing, enabling the user to make an informed purchase decision.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Product Images:**  A carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showing different angles of the laptop (including the screen, keyboard, and ports).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scuffs on the bottom casing)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Small square image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Smith
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8 stars) - (123 Reviews)
    * **Description:** "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor scuffs on the bottom case.  Runs smoothly and battery health is excellent (85%). Comes with original charger.  Specs: 2.7GHz Intel Core i5, 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD."
    * **Specifications:**
        * **Processor:** 2.7GHz Intel Core i5
        * **RAM:** 8GB
        * **Storage:** 256GB SSD
        * **OS:** macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:** Add to Watchlist (Button)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Listing Updated:**  "Last updated: 3 hours ago"
