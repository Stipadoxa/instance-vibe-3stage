## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** To view the details of a used laptop listing and potentially contact the seller to purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive details about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to assess its condition, price, and seller reliability before initiating contact.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**
    * **Gallery:**  (Carousel of at least 3 high-quality photos showing different angles of the laptop, including any visible wear and tear).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scratches on the case, fully functional)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Avatar:** [Image of Seller Profile Picture]
        * **Username:**  @TechSavvySeller
        * **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8 stars)  (12 reviews)
        * **Member Since:**  June 2018
    * **Description:**  "Used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch, in excellent condition.  Minor scratches on the case, barely noticeable.  Battery life is still great.  Comes with original charger.  Always kept in a protective sleeve.  Upgrading to a newer model."
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**
    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:** Add to Wishlist (Button), Report Listing (Button - small, discreet)


* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * "Last updated: 2 hours ago" (Timestamp of last modification to the listing)
    * If applicable:  Shipping Information (Free shipping, or specify shipping cost and method)



## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Mobile App Navigation:**

* **Top Navigation:**
    * Back (Icon: Left-pointing arrow)

* **Bottom Navigation:**
    * Home (Icon: House)
    * Search (Icon: Magnifying glass)
    * Messages (Icon: Speech bubble)
    * Profile (Icon: Person)

**Note:**  The "Report Listing" button could potentially open a modal, with no change to the main bottom navigation.  If the user adds the item to their wishlist, the "Wishlist" tab (if it exists) should update to reflect that change.
