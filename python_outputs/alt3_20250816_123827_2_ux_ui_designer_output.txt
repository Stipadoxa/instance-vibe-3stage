Caption/Small presents the last updated timestamp.

- **Spacing System**:  The `itemSpacing` of 16px within the content container provides a comfortable visual separation between the different sections of information.  Padding of 16px on each side creates comfortable margins within the content area.

- **Component Behavior**: The image carousel (`componentNodeId: 10:7816`) displays multiple product images. The "Contact Seller" button is a primary action and utilizes a "Large" size, while the "Report Listing" button is less prominent and smaller.

- **Color Application**:  Primary/primary50 (green) is used for the main CTA button ("Contact Seller") to highlight its importance. Neutral colors are used consistently for text, backgrounds, and less prominent elements.

- **Layout Strategy**: A vertical layout is employed for the main content to reflect the natural flow of information from top to bottom. The Specifications section uses a vertical layout for the bulleted list. A horizontal layout is used for price and last updated date to present them as a single unit.


## Domain Context Integration

- **Industry Patterns**: The design follows e-commerce conventions: a prominent product image, clear pricing, detailed specifications, and prominent call to action to contact the seller.

- **User Mental Models**: The vertical layout and clear sectioning helps users easily scan the details. The prominence of the price and the contact button are key to conversion.
