## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** To view the details of a used laptop listing and potentially contact the seller to purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display detailed information about a used laptop for sale, allowing the user to assess its condition and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro - 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**
    * **Gallery:** (Carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showing different angles of the laptop, including any visible wear and tear.  First image should be a hero shot.)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (minor scratches on the bottom casing)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of Seller Profile Picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Smith
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8 out of 5 stars based on 27 reviews)
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * Memory: 8GB RAM
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used MacBook Pro in excellent condition.  Always kept in a case.  Battery health is at 92%. Includes original charger."
    * **Shipping:** Free Shipping within the US.


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**
    * **Primary Action:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary Action:**  Report Listing (Small, discreet button in the top right corner)


* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * **Listing Updated:**  "Last updated: 2 hours ago"
