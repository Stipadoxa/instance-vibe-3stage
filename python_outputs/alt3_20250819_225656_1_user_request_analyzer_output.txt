## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain**: E-commerce
2. **Platform**: Mobile
3. **User Goal**: The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose**: To display all relevant information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed decision about whether to contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions**:

* **Heading**:  2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks**:
    * **Image Carousel:**  (Four high-quality images of the laptop from different angles, showing its condition.)  Image 1: Laptop closed. Image 2: Laptop open showing keyboard. Image 3: Close-up of screen. Image 4: Ports and side view.
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (minor scratches on the bottom casing)
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey
    * **Seller Profile:**  (Includes profile picture, username, and rating)  Seller:  @techsavvyseller (4.8 stars, 127 reviews)
    * **Description:**  "This MacBook Pro is in excellent condition and has been meticulously cared for.  Battery health is at 92%. Includes original charger.  Selling due to upgrade."
    * **Shipping Information:**  Free Shipping within the US.


* **Primary & Secondary Actions**:
    * **Primary Action:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary Action:** Add to Favorites (Heart icon)


* **Required Status Indicators**:
    * "Listed 2 days ago" (Timestamp indicating listing age)
