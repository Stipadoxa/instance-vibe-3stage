## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive information about a used laptop for sale, enabling the user to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Gallery:**  (Carousel of at least 3 high-quality photos of the laptop from different angles, including one showing the laptop's condition)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (minor scratches on the bottom case)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Smith
        * **Seller Rating:** 4.8 stars (123 reviews)
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13" in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor scratches on the bottom case.  Runs smoothly, battery health is at 85%. Includes original charger.  Asking $450."
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  Report Listing (Button - smaller and less prominent)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Listing Updated:** Last updated: 2 hours ago
