## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before deciding whether to contact the seller.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display comprehensive details of a used laptop listing, allowing the user to assess its condition, price, and seller reliability before contacting the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro - 13-inch - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Image Carousel:**  (Four high-quality images showing different angles of the laptop, including the keyboard and screen.)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scratches on the bottom casing)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Username:**  @TechSavvySeller
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5, 123 reviews)
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition, minor cosmetic scratches on the bottom.  Battery health is at 85%. Includes original charger. Runs smoothly and is ready to use.  "
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary Action:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary Action:**  View Seller Profile (Link to seller's profile page)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Last Updated:** Updated 2 hours ago


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Top Navigation:**

* **Back:** (Icon and text) - Navigates to previous screen (likely search results or category page).

**Bottom Navigation:**

* **Home:** (Icon and text) -  Navigates to the main marketplace home screen.
* **Search:** (Icon and text) - Navigates to the search screen.
* **Notifications:** (Icon and text) - Navigates to notifications feed.
* **Profile:** (Icon and text) -  Navigates to the user's profile page.

**Note**:  No data scenarios would require appropriate messaging, such as "No images available" for the image carousel or "No reviews yet" for seller rating.  The "Contact Seller" button would remain, though messaging could adapt for inactive sellers.
