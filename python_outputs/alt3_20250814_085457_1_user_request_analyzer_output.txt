## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** To view the details of a used laptop listing and potentially contact the seller to purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display comprehensive details of a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed purchasing decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Images:**  Carousel of at least 4 high-quality photos of the laptop (showing different angles, keyboard, ports etc.).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (minor scuffs on the bottom case)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * Profile Picture: [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * Seller Name: John Doe
        * Seller Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8 stars)  (123 reviews)
    * **Description:** "Used 2015 MacBook Pro 13" in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor scuffs on the bottom case.  Fully functional and comes with original charger.  Runs smoothly and battery health is at 85%.  Specs: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5, 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD."
    * **Specifications Table:** (collapsible for mobile view)
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey
        * Battery Health: 85%
    * **Shipping Information:** Free shipping within the US.  Estimated delivery: 3-5 business days.


* **Primary Actions:**
    * Contact Seller (Button)


* **Secondary Actions:**
    *  Report Listing (Button) (leads to a form)


* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * Last Updated:  October 26, 2023
