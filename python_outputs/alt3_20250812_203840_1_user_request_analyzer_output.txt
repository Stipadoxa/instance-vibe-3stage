## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce (marketplace, product, seller)
2. **Platform:** Unclear, but defaults to mobile in Stage 3.
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing before potentially purchasing it from a seller on a marketplace.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display comprehensive details of a used laptop listing, allowing the user to assess its condition, seller reputation, and make a purchase decision.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**
    * **Product Images:** Carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showcasing different angles of the laptop (including screen, keyboard, ports).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition Details:** "Excellent condition. Minor scratches on the bottom casing. Fully functional."
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Doe
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 out of 5 stars)  (123 reviews)
    * **Description:** "Selling my well-maintained MacBook Pro.  It's been used primarily for light tasks such as web browsing and document editing. Battery health is at 85%. Includes original charger.  No box."
    * **Specifications:** (Table format)
        * **Processor:** 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * **RAM:** 8GB
        * **Storage:** 256GB SSD

* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**
    * **Primary:** "Contact Seller" (button)
    * **Secondary:** "Report Listing" (button - smaller and less prominent)

* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * "Listed 2 days ago" (smaller text below the heading)
