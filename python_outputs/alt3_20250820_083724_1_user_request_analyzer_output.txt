## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before deciding whether to contact the seller.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive details of a used laptop listing, allowing the user to assess its condition, price, and seller credibility before contacting the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Image Carousel:**  Four high-quality images of the laptop (showing different angles, keyboard, ports).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scratches on the bottom casing)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * Profile Picture: [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * Seller Name: John Smith
        * Seller Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5, 123 reviews)
        * **Shipping:** Free Shipping (US only)
    * **Description:**  "This 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch is in excellent condition. It has been meticulously maintained and comes with the original charger.  It's perfect for students or anyone looking for a reliable laptop.  The battery life is still very good (around 8 hours).  Minor scratches on the bottom casing are barely noticeable."
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:** Add to Favorites (Button)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * Last Updated: 2 hours ago


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Top Navigation:**

* Back (Icon: Left-pointing arrow)

**Bottom Navigation:**

* Home (Icon: House)
* Search (Icon: Magnifying glass)
* Favorites (Icon: Heart)
