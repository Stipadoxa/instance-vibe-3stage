## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive details about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro - 13-inch - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**
    * **Image Carousel:**  (Four high-quality images showing different angles of the laptop, including the screen, keyboard, and ports.  Images should be zoomable.)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (minor scratches on the case)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Smith
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5 stars)  (123 reviews)
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * Memory: 8GB RAM
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor cosmetic scratches on the case. Battery health is at 85%.  Includes original charger.  Runs smoothly and has always been well-maintained."
    * **Shipping Information:** Free shipping within the US.

* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**
    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button that initiates a messaging system)
    * **Secondary:** Add to Favorites (heart icon)


* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * **Listing Updated:** Last updated: 2 hours ago


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Mobile App Navigation:**

* **Top Navigation:**
    * Back (left-aligned, arrow icon)
    * Share (right-aligned, share icon)

* **Bottom Navigation:**
    * Home (house icon)
    * Search (magnifying glass icon)
    * Messages (speech bubble icon)
    * Profile (person icon)
