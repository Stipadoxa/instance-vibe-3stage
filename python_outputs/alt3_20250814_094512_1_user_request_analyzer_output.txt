## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app to determine if they want to purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display comprehensive information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to decide whether to contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Images:** Carousel of at least 4 high-quality photos of the laptop (showing different angles, keyboard, etc.).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent - Minor scratches on the bottom casing. Fully functional.
    * **Seller Profile:**  
        * **Avatar:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Username:**  @TechSavvySeller
        * **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5 stars) (123 reviews)
    * **Description:** "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor cosmetic wear on the bottom.  Includes original charger.  Runs smoothly, battery health at 85%.  Upgraded to 16GB RAM."
    * **Specifications:** (Presented as a collapsible section)
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 16GB
        * Storage: 512GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  Add to Wishlist (Button), Report Listing (Button)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Listing Status:**  Active (Could be a small icon or text)
    * **Last Updated:** Updated 2 hours ago


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Top Navigation:**

* Back (Icon: Left-pointing arrow)


**Bottom Navigation:**

* Home (Icon: House)
* Search (Icon: Magnifying glass)
* Wishlist (Icon: Heart)
* Profile (Icon: Person)
