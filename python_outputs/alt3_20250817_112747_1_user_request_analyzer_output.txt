## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display comprehensive details of a used laptop listing, allowing the user to assess its condition, features, and seller reliability before deciding whether to contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**
    * **Image Carousel:**  Four high-quality images of the laptop from different angles (showing the screen, keyboard, ports, etc.).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent - Minor scratches on the bottom casing. Fully functional.
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Doe
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5 stars)  (123 reviews)
    * **Specifications:**
        * **Processor:** 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * **RAM:** 8GB
        * **Storage:** 256GB SSD
        * **Operating System:** macOS Monterey
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used MacBook Pro in excellent condition.  Battery health is at 85%. Includes original charger.  Selling because I upgraded to a newer model."
    * **Shipping Information:** Free shipping within the US.

* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**
    * **Primary Action:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary Action:** Add to Favorites (Button/Icon)


* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * **Listing Updated:**  "Last updated: 2 hours ago"
