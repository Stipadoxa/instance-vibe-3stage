## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** To view the details of a used laptop listing and potentially contact the seller to purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display all relevant information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed decision about purchasing it.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro - 13-inch - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Gallery:**  (Carousel of at least 3 high-quality images of the laptop from different angles.  Include a main image, followed by secondary images accessible via swipe or tap gestures.)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scratches on the bottom case. Fully functional.)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Circular Profile Picture of the seller]
        * **Seller Name:** John Doe
        * **Seller Rating:** 4.8 stars (125 reviews)
    * **Specifications:**
        * **Processor:** 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * **RAM:** 8GB
        * **Storage:** 256GB SSD
        * **Operating System:** macOS Monterey (Upgradable)
    * **Description:** "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro in excellent condition.  Battery health is at 85%.  Comes with original charger."
    * **Shipping Information:** Free shipping within the US.


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary Action:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary Actions:**
        * Add to Watchlist (Button) (Adds the item to a user's saved list)
        * Report Listing (Button) (Opens a form to report inappropriate content)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Last Updated:** Updated 2 hours ago
