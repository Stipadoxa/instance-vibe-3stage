# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Component Intelligence Applied

- **Typography Hierarchy**:  Headline/Large is used for the main product title to ensure prominence. Title/Large and Title/Medium are used for section headers (Specifications, Description), and Body/Large and Body/Medium for the supporting text to maintain a clear visual hierarchy.  Caption/Small is used for the "Last updated" timestamp.

- **Spacing System**:  The `itemSpacing` of 16px is used consistently between major content blocks to provide visual breathing room.  Internal padding within components like list items is leveraged as defined in the design system.  Padding within the content container is 16px on the sides for standard horizontal padding.

- **Component Behavior**: The image carousel leverages a component designed for multiple images.  The "Button" component is used for the "Contact Seller" CTA, with the "Large" and "Filled" variants selected for optimal visual impact. Icons are selected semantically (arrow for back navigation, heart for favorites, flag for reporting).

- **Color Application**: Primary colors are used sparingly for important actions (the "Contact Seller" button). Neutral colors are primarily used for text and background. This ensures that the visual hierarchy and brand guidelines are maintained.

- **Layout Strategy**: The three-container mobile architecture is strictly followed with top navigation (app bar), content container, and bottom navigation.  The `layoutMode` is set to "VERTICAL" for the main layout to support a top-to-bottom reading pattern.

## Domain Context Integration

- **Industry Patterns**: The design follows standard e-commerce patterns for product listings with a hero image, price, condition, specifications, description, and seller information.

- **User Mental Models**: The layout follows a natural top-to-bottom and left-to-right reading flow, which matches user expectations.  Key information is presented prominently.

- **Contextual Adaptations**: The app bar title is kept concise ("Product Details") while the complete product title is placed prominently in the content area, addressing the title length constraint of the app bar.

- **Component Verification**: All componentNodeIds have been validated against the provided `DESIGN_SYSTEM_DATA`.
