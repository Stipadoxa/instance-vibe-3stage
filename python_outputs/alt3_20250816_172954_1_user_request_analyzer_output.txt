## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display comprehensive details of a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro - 13-inch - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**
    * **Image Carousel:**  Four high-quality images of the laptop from different angles (showing keyboard, screen, ports, etc.).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scratches on the bottom case)
    * **Seller Profile:**  (Image of seller profile picture)  John Smith (4.8 stars, 123 reviews)
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor scratches on the bottom case.  Includes original charger.  Runs macOS Ventura smoothly.  Battery health at 85%."
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Ventura


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**
    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  View Seller Profile (Link)

* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * "Last updated: 2 hours ago"


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Top Navigation:**
* Back (Icon: Left-pointing arrow)

**Bottom Navigation:**
* Home (Icon: House)
* Search (Icon: Magnifying glass)
* Messages (Icon: Speech bubble)
* Profile (Icon: Person)
