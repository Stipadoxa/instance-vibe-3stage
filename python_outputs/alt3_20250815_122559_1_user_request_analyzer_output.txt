## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** To view the details of a used laptop listing and potentially contact the seller to purchase it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display all relevant information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to decide whether to contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:**  2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Images:** Carousel of at least 3 high-quality photos of the laptop (showing different angles, keyboard, etc.).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scuffs on the bottom casing)
    * **Seller Profile:**  
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Smith
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (123 reviews)
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch.  Excellent condition with minor scuffs on the bottom.  Runs smoothly and quickly. Includes original charger.  Battery health at 85%. Specs: 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD, Intel Core i5."
    * **Specifications:**
        * **Processor:** Intel Core i5
        * **RAM:** 8GB
        * **Storage:** 256GB SSD
        * **Operating System:** macOS Monterey


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  Share Listing (Button)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Listing Posted:** Listed 3 days ago
