## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To display comprehensive details of a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro - 13-inch - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Product Images:** Carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showcasing different angles of the laptop (e.g., open lid showing keyboard, closed lid, side profile showing ports, close-up of any imperfections).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scratches on the bottom casing)
    * **Seller Profile:**  (Image of Seller Profile Picture)  John Doe  (4.8/5 stars rating) (127 reviews)
    * **Description:** "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor scratches on the bottom.  Includes original charger.  Specs: 2.7GHz Intel Core i5, 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD.  Battery health 85%."
    * **Specifications Table:**  (Table format with columns for Spec and Value.  Examples: Processor, RAM, Storage, Battery Health, Screen Size, Operating System)
    * **Shipping Information:** Free Shipping within the US.  (Note: If shipping isn't free, specific details MUST be provided.  Example: $20 Shipping to Continental US)


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  Add to Favorites (Button, Heart Icon)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * "Listed 3 days ago"  (Status text indicating listing age)


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Top Navigation:**

* Back (Left-aligned icon and text)


**Bottom Navigation:**

* Home (House Icon)
* Search (Magnifying Glass Icon)
* Messages (Chat Bubble Icon)
* Profile (Person Icon)

**Note:**  If the "Add to Favorites" action is used heavily in the app, it should be considered for permanent placement in the bottom navigation.
