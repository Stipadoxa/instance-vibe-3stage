## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed decision about purchasing it.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Product Images:**  A carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showcasing different angles of the laptop (including the screen, keyboard, ports, and any minor wear and tear).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (minor scratches on the bottom casing)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:**  [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Doe
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8 stars)  (123 reviews)
    * **Description:**  "Lightly used 2015 MacBook Pro 13" in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor scratches on the bottom.  Includes original charger.  Battery health is at 85%.  Runs macOS Ventura smoothly.  Selling because I upgraded to a newer model."
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  Report Listing (Button), Add to Wishlist (Button)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * **Listing Updated:** Last updated: 3 hours ago
