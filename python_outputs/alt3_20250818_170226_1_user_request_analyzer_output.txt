## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**
    * **Product Images:** Carousel of at least 4 high-quality images showcasing different angles of the laptop (including screen, keyboard, ports).
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent - Minor scratches on the bottom case. Fully functional.
    * **Seller Profile:**  Avatar Image of seller John Doe  (4.8 rating (123 reviews))
    * **Description:** "Selling my well-maintained 2015 MacBook Pro 13".  Includes original charger. Battery life is excellent.  Runs macOS Ventura smoothly.  Some minor cosmetic wear and tear as shown in pictures."
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Ventura


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**
    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:**  Add to Wishlist (Button)

* **Required Status Indicators:**
    * "Listed 3 days ago"


## STAGE 3: ADD NAVIGATION

**Mobile App Navigation:**

* **Top Navigation:**
    * Back (Icon: Left-pointing arrow)

* **Bottom Navigation:**
    * Home (Icon: House)
    * Search (Icon: Magnifying glass)
    * Messages (Icon: Speech bubble)
    * Profile (Icon: Person)
