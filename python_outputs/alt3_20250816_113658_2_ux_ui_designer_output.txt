# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Component Intelligence Applied

- **Typography Hierarchy**: The headline uses `Headline/Large` for prominence,  `Headline/Medium` for the price, and `Body/Medium` for descriptive text.  `Caption/Small` is used for less important information like the last updated timestamp.  This follows a clear visual hierarchy guiding the user's attention.

- **Spacing System**: `itemSpacing` of 16 is used between major content sections to provide visual breathing room and separation.  Padding is used consistently within containers (16px on sides, 20px top/bottom) to create a comfortable reading experience and maintain visual harmony with the design system.

- **Component Behavior**: The image gallery leverages the `IMAGES` component for efficient image display. The button variants (`Size`, `Type`, `Color`) are chosen to reflect the importance of each action â€“  "Contact Seller" being most prominent with large filled button.  The seller profile utilizes the `avatar / single` and  `icon/star_rate` components for a unified visual style, providing visual cues regarding seller trustworthiness.

- **Color Application**:  Primary colors (`Primary/primary50`) are used for prominent actions (CTAs) and prices. Neutral colors (`Neutral/neutral90` and `Neutral/neutral50`) are used consistently for text to improve readability and maintain consistency with the design system.

- **Layout Strategy**:  The three-container mobile app architecture is strictly followed: top navigation (app bar), content area, and bottom navigation.  The vertical layout ensures a natural top-to-bottom flow of information, enhancing readability and improving usability.


## Domain Context Integration

- **Industry Patterns**: The design incorporates common e-commerce patterns such as product image carousels, pricing, seller ratings, and clear CTAs.  The layout follows typical mobile shopping app conventions, making it intuitive for users familiar with similar apps.

- **User Mental Models**: The design uses a top-to-bottom, left-to-right flow, which matches most users' mental models for reading and navigating interfaces. The grouping of related content into visually distinct blocks enhances the user's comprehension.


- **Contextual Adaptations**: The `appbar` component is modified with visibility overrides to include only the back button in the top navigation.  The full product title is placed prominently in the main content area, ensuring it's not cut off in the app bar.
