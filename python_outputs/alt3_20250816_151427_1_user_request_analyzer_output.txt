## STAGE 1: UNDERSTAND THE REQUEST

1. **Domain:** E-commerce
2. **Platform:** Mobile
3. **User Goal:** The user wants to view the details of a used laptop listing on a marketplace app before potentially purchasing it.


## STAGE 2: GENERATE CORE CONTENT

**Main Screen Purpose:** To provide comprehensive information about a used laptop listing, allowing the user to make an informed purchase decision and contact the seller.

**Required Information & Actions:**

* **Heading:** 2015 MacBook Pro 13" - Space Gray - Excellent Condition

* **Key Information Blocks:**

    * **Image Carousel:**  (4 high-quality images showing different angles of the laptop, including keyboard and screen.)
    * **Price:** $450
    * **Condition:** Excellent (Minor scratches on the bottom case, fully functional)
    * **Seller Profile:**
        * **Profile Picture:** [Image of seller's profile picture]
        * **Seller Name:** John Doe
        * **Seller Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8 stars)  (120 ratings)
    * **Specifications:**
        * Processor: 2.7 GHz Intel Core i5
        * RAM: 8GB
        * Storage: 256GB SSD
        * Operating System: macOS Monterey
    * **Description:** "Lightly used MacBook Pro 13-inch in Space Gray.  Excellent condition with minor cosmetic wear.  Battery health is at 90%. Includes original charger.  Selling due to upgrade."
    * **Shipping Information:** Free shipping within the US.


* **Primary & Secondary Actions:**

    * **Primary:** Contact Seller (Button)
    * **Secondary:** Add to Wishlist (Button)


* **Required Status Indicators:**

    * "Last updated: 2 hours ago"
